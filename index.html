COLE



Hello there! My name is Cole Snyder and I am a front end developer from the midwest with an appetite for good design and elegant code. I'm an engaged creator looking to leverage my coding skills. In my freetime I like to draw, cook and take advantage of the midwest environment by swimming, hiking and exercising. My personal philosophy that everyone should have three hobbies: one to keep you creative, one to keep you in shape and one to make you money. Swimming, hiking and weight lifting are my hobbies that keep me in shape, drawing is my hobby for keeping myself creative and programming is my hobby for making myself money. I enjoy all three equally and think it's a great way to stay happy and successful.


PROGRAMMING



Since 2014 programming has been a hobby of mine. I really enjoy writing code and thinking critically as a way of keeping my mind sharp. I am most spirited about front end development and can't wait to get out there and start creating products for the world to see.


PHOTOREALISM DRAWING



Photorealism drawing is a hobby of mine. I like to draw large portraits of famous people. To make my work unique I record myself drawing the portraits with a tablet and after about 9 hours of drawing it's finished. I would take the finished video and speed it up 64x and add my favorite music to it. The result is the drawings on my YouTube page. I had a great time making them and I hope you enjoy them too! - ColeSnyderArt
